We are upgrading your CartoDB

CartoDB is doing system maintenance where we are updating some of the underlying infrastructure. Hope this isn't too much of an inconvenience but we are sure the results will be worth it!

Your account is one that we are currently in the middle of working on. During this time, CartoDB will be moving your data and in the process will not be able to accept any write or update requests (including through the SQL API), therefore your dashboard is temporarily unavailable. You should have received notice of this through the email linked to your account and through our newsletter. All of your published visualizations will continue to work as expected so your maps should experience zero downtime.
